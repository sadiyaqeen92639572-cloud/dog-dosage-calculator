# Dog Medication Dosage Calculator by Weight

**[→ Open the Live Calculator](https://sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/dog-dosage-calculator/)**

Free interactive tool to calculate common dog medication dosages by weight. Enter your dog's weight in lbs or kg and get instant dosage ranges for the most prescribed canine medications — no vet visit required for reference.

## Medications Covered

- **Benadryl (Diphenhydramine)** — allergies, itching, anxiety, motion sickness
- **Gabapentin** — nerve pain, seizures, pre-surgical anxiety
- **Trazodone** — behavioral anxiety, thunderstorm phobia, travel stress
- **Amoxicillin** — bacterial infections (skin, respiratory, urinary tract)
- **Carprofen (Rimadyl)** — joint pain, post-operative pain, osteoarthritis
- **Meloxicam** — inflammation, chronic pain management
- **Metronidazole (Flagyl)** — digestive issues, diarrhea, giardia
- **Prednisone / Prednisolone** — allergic reactions, immune conditions, inflammation

## How to Use

1. Go to **[sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/dog-dosage-calculator/](https://sadiyaqeen92639572-cloud.github.io/dog-dosage-calculator/)**
2. Enter your dog's weight (lbs or kg)
3. Select the medication
4. Read the recommended dosage range with safety notes

Dosage ranges are based on standard veterinary references. Always confirm with your vet before administering any medication.

## Why This Tool Exists

Standard dosage charts online are static tables with no weight input — you have to do the math yourself, often under stress. This calculator does the math instantly and flags safety caps (e.g., Benadryl max per dose regardless of weight, Gabapentin renal caution).
